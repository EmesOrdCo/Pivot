Features

Hidden Sharpener

Hidden Metal Sharpener – Tucked neatly into the bottom of the handle, this high-quality sharpener stays protected under a sliding lid. Ready when you need it, invisible when you don't.

Infinite Pencil

Screw-In Infinite Pencil Nib – A compact graphite nib that twists securely into the base of the handle. When not in use, it tucks away neatly in a compartment secured behind a sliding lid.

Detachable Ruler

Detachable Ruler – A slim, lightweight ruler that clips securely into the handle for easy access. Detach it when needed, then snap it back into place for compact, on-the-go measuring.

Removable Eraser

Removable Eraser Tip – A compact eraser that slots neatly into the handle, ready when you need it and easy to replace when worn. Designed for clean lines and clean storage.
